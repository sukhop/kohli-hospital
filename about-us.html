Vision

To develop super/sub speciality for spinal disorders for upcoming challenges of ‘Age Quake’. Prevention and treatment of chronic spinal pain disorders by developing life style modification centre

Sub-specialty Surgical to develop – Cervical surgery, deformity, endoscopy, MISS and Oncospine surgery. Sub-specialty work has been delegated since last two years.

Clinical Sub-specialty to develop – Ankylosing, Osteoporosis and Deformity.

OPD Facilities

Stavya has five full time spine surgeons, two fulltime post MS fellows (create hyperlink of word ‘spine surgeon’) and a team of 15 dedicated physiotherapists who take care of assessment & educate patients. Stavya has efficient management team who handles nonclinical work. OPD functions six days a week, Monday to Saturday. Individual spine surgeons can be consulted with prior appointment. (create hyperlink) Patient can book appointment by our website or on call or through the personal appointment. Those needy patients who walk-in without prior appointment are catered care by our team members. Important aspect is LIFE style Modification, the conservative group of patients are advised to visit the centre, THE BODY & THE MIND. This centre is led by trained physiotherapist and fitness consultant.
